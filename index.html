About

Death

Baji Keisuke died on the Bloody Halloween by stabbing himself. While dying, He revealed to takemichi and chifuyu that the real Enemy is Kisaki Tetta.
His last words were:

"Pah-chin, Mitsuya, Draken, Mikey, Kazutora. Toman's founding members are my treasure. I wanted to fix this on my own. But, I just couldn't. I died on my own."
